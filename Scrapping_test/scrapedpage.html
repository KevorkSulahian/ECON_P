Job Description
Vacancy Announcement
Psychologist
The International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) is an impartial, neutral and independent organisation with the exclusively humanitarian mission to protect the lives and dignity of victims of war and internal violence and to provide them with assistance. It also endeavors to prevent suffering by promoting and strengthening International Humanitarian Law and universal humanitarian principles. Established in 1863, the ICRC is at the origin of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement.
The ICRC Delegation in Armenia is announcing a vacant full-time position of “Psychologist”, based in Yerevan.


Job Responsibilities
-The Psychologist will be supporting operational activities related to ICRC’s psychological programmes according to the objectives set out in the Delegation
-Supports in preparing joint assessments of affected villages and/or communities to reveal psychological threats to the population
-Carries out psychological support directly through individual consultations or group support meetings in the identified areas
-Contributes to the implementation and creation of new projects, as well as actively participates in the strategic developments of delegation's psychological activities
-Participates in analysis of and reflection on the overall psychological situation in the country
-Creates and maintains direct links with psychological programme beneficiaries, as well as Armenian Red Cross Society in capacity building initiatives
-Organizes respective training sessions whenever required based on the set strategies


Required Qualifications
-University degree in clinical psychology (at least 6 years of a university study)
-At least 3-5 years of relevant work experience, international humanitarian work experience will be viewed as an asset
-Excellent knowledge of spoken and written English, Russian, Armenian
-Computer proficiency
-Excellent organizational skills, ability to take initiative and to work independently
-Teaching/training experience and skills will be viewed as assets
-Disciplined and accurate personality
-Advanced communication and negotiation skills


Application Procedure
Deadline for application: 15 December 2020
In case your skills and background correspond to the mentioned requirements, please send your CV in English, a motivation letter in English and copies of diplomas/certificates to the e-mail ere_hrrecruitment_services@icrc.org with the subject line indicating “Psychologist for Yerevan”.
Please note that only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
We offer on-going development, diverse culture and constant quest to do things better.
For additional information, please consult the ICRC website: www.icrc.org
Please, clearly mention that you have heard about this position from JobFinder.am career portal.
